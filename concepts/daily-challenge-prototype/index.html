Concept Prototype

Daily Challenge + Leaderboard Signup

This is a standalone click-through mock. It shows how a guest can play first, then claim a score to appear on the daily leaderboard.

What we are testing

  • The game stays playable without forced signup.
  • The leaderboard feels valuable enough to justify sign-in.
  • The name-selection step feels like claiming an achievement, not filling a form.

Flow logic

Guest plays the challenge, sees the score, and only then gets the prompt to save it. Official leaderboard entry happens after sign-in and display-name selection.
